## About Me

Jin Huang is a Ph.D. candidate in Ocean Engineering at Zhejiang University, supervised by [Prof. Ying Chen](https://person.zju.edu.cn/en/0089096).
His research interests include marine robotics, underwater navigation, and acoustic positioning. 
He is the primary contributor and maintainer of the MUINS (Multi-Sensors Underwater Integrated Navigation System) project, which integrates piUSBL, DVL, Depth Gauge, GNSS, and SINS into real-time navigation algorithms and simulation tools. 
He is also the lead developer of the Raspberry Pi-based passive inverted ultra-short baseline (RaspiUSBL) acoustic positioning system, under the supervision of [Dr. Yingqiang Wang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=_0x40UYAAAAJ&hl=en).
